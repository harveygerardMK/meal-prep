# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A private two-adult household sharing one meal planner on phones and the kitchen counter. Primary job on most screens: decide this week’s dinners and kid lunches, then leave with a grocery list they can shop against. Secondary jobs: grow a trusted recipe catalog, pull a TikTok idea into next week after a quick review, and hand off shopping via Instacart/Google search links.

## Product Purpose

Replace scattered texts, screenshots, and memory with one calm weekly plan. Success looks like: both adults open the app mid-week without confusion, trust what’s cooking, adjust effort/novelty when life is busy, and finish shopping without rebuilding the list elsewhere.

## Brand Personality

Calm, clear, capable. Quiet confidence over cheerleading. Speaks in plain household language (“this week,” “lock dinner,” “copy list”), not meal-kit marketing or food-blog voice.

## Anti-references

- Meal-kit / grocery-delivery marketing pages (hero food photography, lifestyle slogans, conversion CTAs)
- SaaS dashboards with metric strips, empty charts, and “insights” chrome
- Recipe-social clutter (infinite feeds, likes, creator branding)
- Soft cream/sand “AI food app” skins and terracotta-serif editorial kitsch

## Design Principles

1. **Week first** — The current plan is the hero; everything else is a supporting path.
2. **One decision at a time** — Prefer progressive disclosure over one crowded control surface.
3. **Trust through clarity** — Show what’s locked, what’s regenerating, and what will hit the grocery list.
4. **Phone-thumb native** — Primary actions stay reachable and readable on a small screen at the store or stove.
5. **Capable, not clever** — Familiar controls beat novel UI; personality shows in copy and hierarchy, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG 2.2 AA on text contrast, focus visibility, and form labels. Support `prefers-reduced-motion`. Assume shared phones, glare in kitchens/stores, and one-handed use — touch targets ≥44px and high-contrast body text over muted gray.
