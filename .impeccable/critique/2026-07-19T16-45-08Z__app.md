## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: Partial AI-default product UI — zinc bordered cards, rounded-full pills, Geist/Arial unfinished tokens, equal-weight sections. Copy often earns household trust; primitives (lock/regenerate) are real.

**Deterministic scan (CLI)**: 1 warning — `overused-font` on `app/globals.css` (Arial fallback; Geist loaded in layout). Exit findings sparse.

**Browser detect.js**: 2 warnings on `body` — Overused font; Single font for everything. For product register, single-family is often correct (false-positive risk). Injection succeeded; title set to `[Human]`.

## Overall Impression

The product knows the right verbs but fails the 8-second household glance: mix sliders and Regenerate dominate; dinners lack days; grocery sprawls on the plan page while Shop is a secondary pill.

## What's Working

1. Lock + Regenerate is the right shared-week primitive; amber Locked is readable.
2. Copy often matches calm/clear/capable (“This Week’s Plan”, “Copy list”, import review promise).
3. Login stays quiet and appropriate for a household password.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] No day / “tonight” on dinners
- **Why**: Primary job (“what’s for dinner tonight?”) fails on `/`.
- **Fix**: Label Mon–Sun slots; pin Tonight/Up next; collapse ingredient lists.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable layout app/page.tsx`

### [P1] Plan page is one crowded control surface
- **Why**: Mix + dinners + lunches + grocery + 5 header actions compete.
- **Fix**: Lead with dinners; collapse mix; move grocery checkoffs to Shop; make Shop the store primary.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable distill app/page.tsx`

### [P1] Regenerate / lock trust gap
- **Why**: Destructive reshuffle without preview; Lock looks committed prematurely.
- **Fix**: Confirm “will reshuffle X”; clarify locks apply on regenerate; undo/snapshot.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable harden app/page.tsx`

### [P2] Navigation inconsistency & missing shell
- **Why**: Catalog≠Recipes; Shopping omits destinations; no active state.
- **Fix**: Sticky Plan · Shop · Recipes · More with active state.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable shape app shell nav`

### [P2] Destructive Archive with no guardrail
- **Why**: Easy mis-tap on tiny pills.
- **Fix**: Confirm + undo; ≥44px targets.
- **Suggested command**: `/impeccable harden app/recipes/page.tsx`
